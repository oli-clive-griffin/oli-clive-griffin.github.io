Identity and Values
Sprint 1 Core
Wednesday 2 Septmeber, 2020

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took.

I honestly can't think of a serious ethical decision I've had to make recently. But rather a set of small ones. I've been working in my parents restaurant/cinema over the winter and the busiest days it gets really, really busy. Sometimes when I'd dropped the ball in some way or another - forgetting to put a customer's order through, forgetting to clean a cinema - I'd instinctively tell white lies to cover up for myself. After doing this a few times, I noticed what I was doing and was quite disappointed with myself. I'd always thought I was a radically honest person. but under stress, and when being held accountable by my workmates, I had allowed my standards to slip. since that, and especially since dwelling on this idea while doing this exercise, I've committed to being that same radically honest person I have been in the past
